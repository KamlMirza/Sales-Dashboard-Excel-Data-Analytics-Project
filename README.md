# Sales Dashboard | Excel Data Analytics Project

## Task Objective
The objective of this project was to analyze sales performance, track profit trends, evaluate customer contribution, and present the findings through an interactive and decision-focused dashboard.

## Approach

### 1. Data Cleaning and Structuring
- Processed raw CSV data to ensure consistency in date formats.
- Categorized products into three primary segments:
  - Electronics
  - Furniture
  - Office Supplies
- Calculated profit margins using revenue and cost fields.
- Standardized state and customer name formats for accurate aggregation.

### 2. Pivot Table Analysis
Utilized Pivot Tables to generate structured summaries for:

- Year-over-year sales growth (2020–2025).
- State-wise sales distribution.
- Top-performing customers based on total profit.
- Sub-category performance comparisons.

### 3. Visualization Design
- Funnel Charts to rank category contributions.
- Area and Line Charts to analyze seasonality and monthly trends.
- Map Charts to visualize geographic sales distribution across the United States.
- KPI cards to display Total Sales, Total Profit, and Customer Count.

### 4. Interactivity
- Implemented Dynamic Slicers for:
  - Category
  - Year
- Enabled instant drill-down capability across all visual components.
- Ensured dashboard elements update automatically based on filter selections.

## Results and Key Insights

### Top Revenue Drivers
- The "Markers" and "Tables" sub-categories emerged as the highest revenue contributors.
- Both exceeded $625,000 in total sales.

### Profit Leaders
- Identified the Top 5 most profitable customers.
- Stephanie Manning and Logan Galloway each generated $8,840 in total profit, leading the profitability ranking.

### Growth Trends
- Customer acquisition peaked in 2022 with 288 customers.
- The starting year (2020) recorded 171 customers, indicating strong growth momentum over time.

### Regional Insights
- High-volume sales clusters were observed in specific states.
- These regions present strong opportunities for targeted marketing and expansion strategies.

### Seasonality Patterns
- Monthly area charts revealed recurring peak sales periods.
- Insights enable improved inventory planning and workforce allocation during high-demand months.
